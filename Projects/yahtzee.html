Yahtzee!!

Roll 5 dice by clicking the roll button, you can then click on any dice you want to keep and click reroll to reroll the others (You can double-click on a dice to de-select it). You may reroll up to 3 times. Once you have used up your dice you can click bank score to save your score. This will prompt you to select a category to save it to. Your opponent will reveal their scores as you bank yours.
